Other requirements for employment:
	A master's or doctoral degree in a related discipline may be required.
	
	Licensing by a provincial or territorial association of professional engineers is required
	to approve engineering drawings and reports and to practice as a Professional Engineer (P.Eng.).
	
	Engineers are eligible for registration following graduation from an accredited educational program
	with three or four years of supervised work experience in engineering, and passing a professional practice examination.
	
	Experience as a computer programmer is usually required.

Types of Employers (+Examples):
	Public Sector:
		Eg. Research Analyst for the Center for Sustainable Energy (San Diego, CA)
		Eg. Intelligence Specialist for the U.S Air Force
	Private Sector:
		Eg. Cloud Application Architect for Royal Bank of Canada (RBC)
